Corn Flakes Cheese-Crusted Chicken

  • Servings: 4
  • Prep Time: 75 mins
  • Total Time: 95 mins

Ingredients

  • 10 Chicken drumette
  • Oil for frying
  • For Marinade
  • 2 cm of Ginger, pounded
  • 2 cloves of Garlic, pounded
  • 2 tablespoon of Plain yoghurt
  • 1 tablespoon of Chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of Fennel powder
  • 1 tablespoon of Lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • For coating
  • 2 cups of tablespoon of Kellogg's® Corn Flakes, coarsely crushed
  • 1 Egg, lightly beaten
  • 3/4 cup of Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Mix chicken with marinating ingredients.
  2. Cover and set aside for an hour in the chiller.
  3. Season egg with salt and black pepper
  4. Coat chicken with Parmesan cheese then dip into the egg.
  5. Then coat chicken with Kellogg’s® Corn Flakes.
  6. Fry in hot oil until golden brown and cooked through.
