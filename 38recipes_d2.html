Cheesy Mashed Mueslix® Potatoes

  • Servings: 5
  • Prep Time: 40 mins
  • Total Time: 60 mins

Ingredients

  • 50g of Kellogg's® Mueslix® Raisin & Almond Crunch
  • 750g of Boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 50g of Diced cheddar cheese
  • 50g of Cream cheese
  • 75g of Salted butter
  • 1/4 cups of Milk
  • 2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of Pepper powder
  • For Decoration
  • 50g of Kellogg's® Mueslix® Raisin & Almond Crunch
  • 25g of Melted butter
  • 30g of Shredded cheese

Cooking Steps

  1. Mix the above ingredients in a large bowl until it is well combined.
  2. Pour and flatten the above mixture into a pot then sprinkle the toppings on top.
  3. Bake it at 180 °C for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
