Frosties® Crusted French Toast

  • Servings: 6
  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Total Time: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cups of Kellogg's® Frosties®, lightly crushed
  • 1 Beaten egg
  • 3 tablespoon of Milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon of Salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon of Cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Vanilla
  • 3 Thick-slice sandwich bread

Cooking Steps

  1. Whisk together egg, milk, sugar, salt, cinnamon and vanilla.
  2. Slice each bread diagonally into triangles.
  3. Soak break in the egg mixture for 1 minute or until well coated.
  4. Coat bread with crushed Kellogg’s® Frosties®. Pat crushed cereal onto bread to make sure it adheres well.
  5. Fry coated bread in butter until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Optional: Serve with maple syrup, a dusting of powdered sugar or fruits of choice and enjoy!
