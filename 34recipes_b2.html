Froot Loops® Raya Bar

  • Servings: 9
  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Total Time: 35 mins

Ingredients

  • 180g of Butter
  • 100g of Cream cheese
  • 130g of Icing sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 320g of Plain flour
  • 1 tablespoon of Rice flour
  • 250g of Cream cheese
  • For Decoration
  • White cooking chocolate, melted
  • Kellogg's® Froot Loops®

Cooking Steps

  1. Beat butter, cream cheese, sugar and egg until combined.
  2. Add flour and rice flour. Mix well.
  3. Next, add Kellogg's® Froot Loops® and mix thoroughly.
  4. Press dough into a 9x9 inch baking tin lined with parchment paper. Bake in a preheated oven at 160°C for 25 minutes.
  5. Transfer onto a wire rack to cool. Cut into 3x3cm squares.
  6. Decorate with melted white chocolate and sprinkle Kellogg's® Froot Loops® on top.
