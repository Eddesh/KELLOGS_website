Cheesy Mashed Mueslix® Potatoes

  • Servings: 4
  • Prep Time: 40 mins
  • Total Time: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • For Crust:
  • 2 1/2 tablespoon of Butter
  • 2 1/2 cups of Mini Marshmallows
  • 2/3 cups of Chocolate chips
  • 3 cups of Kellogg's® Coco Pops®
  • 6 eggs
  • For Topping:
  • 2 cups of Mini Marshmallows
  • 1/2 cups of Chocolate chips

Cooking Steps

  1. Line an 8-inch baking pan with parchment paper. Preheat oven or oven toaster at 150°C, broil setting.
  2. In a nonstick pan over low heat, melt together butter, marshmallows and chocolate chips while stirring continuously with a heat-resistant rubber spatula.
  3. Once melted, turn off heat and mix in Kellogg’s® Coco Pops®. Work fast to ensure that everything is well incorporated before the marshmallows set.
  4. Transfer to the lined baking pan and press down to an even, compact layer. Top with marshmallows and chocolate chips. Let cool until completely set.
  5. Broil for 2-3 minutes or until marshmallows are lightly browned and chocolate melted.
  6. Let cool completely before cutting into bars.
