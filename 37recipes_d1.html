Corn Flakes Nutty Banana Cookies

  • Servings: 36
  • Prep Time: 15 mins
  • Total Time: 40 mins

Ingredients

  • Base ingredients
  • 125g of Butter
  • 35g of Icing sugar
  • 1 Egg yolk
  • 1 teaspoon of Banana Essence
  • 220g of All-purpose flour, sifted
  • 60g of Cornflour, sifted
  • Top Ingredients
  • 140g of Light brown sugar
  • 100ml of Honey
  • 200g of Butter
  • 80g of Kellogg's® Banana Corn Flakes, lightly crushed
  • 80g of Dried bananas, lightly crushed
  • 80g of Deseeded dates, big chopped
  • 140g of Roasted cashew nuts, finely chopped
  • 60g of All-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Salt

Cooking Steps

  1. To make the base, beat butter, sugar, yolk and banana essence until combined.
  2. Add other ingredients and knead into a dough.
  3. Press into 8x8 inch baking tin lined with parchment paper. Prick base with a fork.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for 10 minutes.
  5. For the top ingredients, heat sugar, honey and butter until melted. Remove from heat.
  6. Add in remaining ingredients, stir well and quickly press onto the base.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C for 15 minutes. Set aside to cool.
  8. Chill in the chiller before cutting into 4x4cm squares.
